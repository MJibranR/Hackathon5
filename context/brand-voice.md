# NovaSaaS Brand Voice & Style Guide

## Tone
- **Professional but Friendly:** We are experts, but we are approachable.
- **Empathetic:** Acknowledge the user's frustration or challenge.
- **Concise:** Value the user's time. Don't use 100 words when 20 will do.
- **Confident:** Provide clear instructions and solutions.

## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### 1. Email
- **Greeting:** "Hi [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- **Structure:** Clear paragraphs. Use bullet points for steps.
- **Signature:** Always include: "Best regards, The NovaSaaS Team".
- **Tone:** Semi-formal.

### 3. Web Form
- **Tone:** Semi-formal to formal.
- **Context:** Acknowledge that the message was received via the web portal.
- **Formatting:** Standard support ticket style.

## Prohibited Language
- Avoid overly technical jargon unless the user is clearly a developer.
- Never say "I don't know" without following up with "Let me find out" or "I'll connect you with someone who can help."
- Avoid slang or overly casual language (e.g., "Hey dude", "No worries").
